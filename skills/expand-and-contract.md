# Expand & Contract

A skill for turning raw business ideas into structured, actionable plans through
a guided brainstorming dialogue.

## What It Does

Expand & Contract walks you through your business idea systematically — asking the
right questions to help you define exactly what you're building, who it's for,
what to do on day one, and what to leave for later.

It expands your thinking with possibilities, then contracts it into a clear, focused
scope. The result is a one-page business summary you can actually act on.

## How to Use

1. Type `/expand-and-contract` in Claude
2. Describe your business idea in a sentence or two
3. Answer Claude's questions (services, target market, operations, GTM, etc.)
4. Refine each section — expand on what excites you, cut what doesn't
5. Receive a complete business scope document at the end

## The Process

### Phase 1: Expand
Claude generates a wide set of options across every dimension of your business:
- What services/products could you offer?
- Who could your customers be?
- What channels could you use?
- What could your pricing model look like?

### Phase 2: Contract
You select what fits. Claude helps you narrow down:
- What are the must-haves for Day 1?
- What's Phase 2 or later?
- What are you explicitly NOT doing?

### Phase 3: Scope Summary
Claude produces a structured output including:
- Core offering
- Target customer
- Go-to-market approach
- Positioning principles
- Watch-outs and risks
- Visual business overview

## Example

> **User:** `/expand-and-contract` — I want to start a business offering house help
> to the wealthiest families in India.
>
> **Expand & Contract:** What services do you want to offer? *(Lists 12 options)*
>
> **User:** Full-time living staff, estate manager, private chef.
>
> **E&C:** Which GTM/operational approaches are must-haves? *(Lists 8 options)*
>
> **User:** Background verification + police checks, referral-only membership model.
>
> *(After 5–6 exchanges...)*
>
> **Output:** Complete business scope for a luxury household staffing firm targeting
> India's top 1,000 HNI families — with positioning, services, GTM, and clear watch-outs.

## Output Includes

- **The Scope** — What you're building and for whom
- **Core Services** — Prioritized, Day 1 vs. later
- **Positioning Principles** — How you'll stand out
- **GTM Plan** — How you'll acquire your first customers
- **Watch-Outs** — Risks and things to avoid
- **What We're NOT Doing** — Explicit out-of-scope items
- **Visual Business Overview** — A simple diagram of the model

## Best For

- First-time founders with a rough idea
- Side-project builders who want clarity before building
- Pivoting entrepreneurs reassessing direction
- Anyone with "lots of ideas but not sure what to do next"

## Tips

- Don't filter yourself in Phase 1 — let the expansion be wide
- In Phase 2, be ruthless — fewer things done well beats many things done poorly
- Save the final output as your North Star document for the first 90 days
